### Explanation of Tailwind CSS Classes:
- **Flexbox & Centering**:
  - `flex`: Applies flexbox layout.
  - `flex-col`: Flex direction column.
  - `justify-center`: Center items along the main axis.
  - `items-center`: Center items along the cross axis.
- **Sizing**:
  - `min-h-screen`: Minimum height of 100vh (full screen height).
  - `h-1/2`: Height of 50%.
  - `w-full`: Width of 100%.
  - `md:h-screen`: Height of 100vh on medium screens and larger.
  - `md:w-2/3`: Width of 66.67% on medium screens and larger.
  - `w-64`: Width of 16rem.
  - `md:h-72`: Height of 18rem on medium screens and larger.
  - `w-4/5`: Width of 80%.
  - `max-w-sm`: Maximum width of 24rem.
  - `w-48`: Width of 12rem.
- **Padding & Margin**:
  - `p-8`: Padding of 2rem.
  - `p-3`: Padding of 0.75rem.
  - `mb-6`: Margin-bottom of 1.5rem.
  - `mb-4`: Margin-bottom of 1rem.
  - `mb-6`: Margin-bottom of 1.5rem.
- **Borders & Rounding**:
  - `border-2`: Border width of 2px.
  - `border-black`: Black border color.
  - `rounded-lg`: Large border-radius.
- **Backgrounds & Colors**:
  - `bg-cover`: Background size cover.
  - `bg-center`: Background position center.
  - `bg-transparent`: Transparent background.
  - `bg-teal-100`: Light teal background color.
  - `bg-opacity-80`: Background opacity of 80%.
  - `bg-orange-500`: Orange background color.
  - `hover:bg-orange-400`: Lighter orange background on hover.
  - `text-white`: White text color.
  - `text-gray-500`: Gray text color.
- **Text & Fonts**:
  - `text-2xl`: Text size 1.5rem.
  - `font-bold`: Bold font weight.
  - `text-lg`: Text size 1.125rem.
  - `text-xs`: Text size 0.75rem.
  - `text-center`: Centered text.
- **Utility**:
  - `hover:bg-orange-400`: Change background color on hover.
